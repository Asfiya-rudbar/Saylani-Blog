Saylani Mass IT Training Program

The Saylani Mass IT Training Program is designed to provide free IT education to the youth, equipping them with the skills needed to excel in the technology sector.Through this program, we aim to bridge the digital divide and create opportunities for economic empowerment.

Food Distribution Initiatives

Our food distribution initiatives ensure that thousand of individual and families receive nutritious meals daily. We believe that no one should go hungry, and we work trelessely to provide food to thoes in need.

Healthcare Services

Saylani Welfare provides healthcare services through medical camps, clinics, and hospitals. Our goals to offer accessible and affordable healthcare to underserved communities.

Education for All

We believe that education is a fundamental right. Our educational program include schools, scholarships, and adult literacy courses aimed at empowering individuals through knowledge and skills.

Community Development

Our communities development projects focus on improving infrastructure, providing clean water, and supporting small businesses. We strive to create sustainable communities where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.
